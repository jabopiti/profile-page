## Structure
- Hero section: This is the top section of the homepage and the first thing visitors see: my name, short title/tagline, location, brief description, profile photo, call to-action buttons
- About me: This section explains who you are and your background: short biography, career journey, personal values. and shows as tags my expertise and skills.
- Portfolio: One of the most important sections, shown as cards: past products, case studies
- Speaking & Writing: Highlighting and offering my ability to speak and write. Show the topics I speak and write about.
- Contact: Make it easy for people to reach you: "Say hello. Let's connect. Whether it's about product, tech, a speaking invitation, or just to exchange ideas — I'm always up for a good conversation." with links to linkedin, Xing, speakerdeck
- Footer: apppears at the bottom of every page: copyright notice,  Imprint, privacy policy / Datenschutzerklärung
