About

Who I Am

Utility comes from utilization. Nutzen kommt von Nutzung.

With extensive experience in orchestrating large software efforts, I lead distributed, cross-functional teams united under a shared, clear strategy. I've taken zero-to-one products from idea to launch and scaled complex B2B and technical platforms.

My distinct approach blends systems thinking, design thinking, and platform engineering — applying product thinking to the technical landscape to create remarkable user and developer experiences and business capabilities.

Occasionally I speak and write about product and platform strategy and my adventures in socio-technical systems.

Product Leadership

OTA Platform Scale

"From 1 to 10 teams without losing the plot"

Led the product function of an automotive OTA vehicle update platform, scaling from one to ten product managers and teams — growing data, API, embedded, and developer experience workstreams while maintaining team health and delivery performance.

Zero to One

IoT Product Launch

"An IoT product. Four paying customers. 12 months."

Built and launched an IoT battery management product from scratch within budget and on time, with four paying early adopters — combining connected vehicle capabilities, SaaS architecture, and B2B go-to-market from day one.

"250 people. One shared direction."

Designed the organisational and technical strategy for a large European autonomous driving cooperation — applying an Inverse Conway Maneuver to reduce dependencies, then aligning VP-level leadership down to team leads and ramping up 20+ teams.
